About the Author

Justin T. Cole writes little, but impacvtful, stories.

I have always loved a good story. The summer prior to my 10th birthday I made the leap from loving good stories being told to me to enjoying the act of reading.

🎓 Background

Justin studied literature and creative writing before pursuing a career that spans education, publishing, and the arts. His diverse life experience fuels characters with authentic voices and relatable flaws.

✍️ Writing Style

Justin’s books often blur genre boundaries, weaving elements of speculative fiction, contemporary realism, and lyrical introspection. His writing has been praised for its depth, empathy, and courage to ask difficult questions.
