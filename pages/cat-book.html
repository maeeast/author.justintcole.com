The Cat Who Lit Itself on Fire and Other Tales

Genre: Speculative Fiction

About the Book

The Cat Who Lit Itself on Fire and Other Mostly-True, Mostly-Humorous Tales is a collection of 41 autobiographical experiences presented for your amusement.

Unlike my fiction stories, these anecdotes don't contain layers. They are what they are and they are best consumed with a light heart.

All stated dates are to my best recollection and could be in error.

This book is currently with an Editor so word counts and general contents are subject to change between this page and final publication.
